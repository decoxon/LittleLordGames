## The Book

- Good quality, 114-page softcover rulebook.
- Charming tone, almost as conversational as some of Rick Priestly's work such
  as _Hail Caesar!_.

## The Game

- Movement feels quite swingy and strange when you get to move a total of 9"
  across an open field over three activations on 2D6, but this is part of the
  simulationist nature of the game. Playing at a smaller scale to match the
  ground scale may reduce this feeling.
